1) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide

Account information: email and basic account identifiers.

User inputs and wellness data: meals you log; meal labels (e.g., breakfast/lunch/dinner); goals you select; self-ratings (e.g., energy/sleep); and other information you enter (such as weight, if you choose to log it).

Support communications: information you include if you contact us.

B. Photo and voice inputs (if you use those features)

Meal photos: if you use photo logging, you may upload a photo to be analyzed to create a meal log and nutrition estimates.

We do not store meal photos on our servers.

Voice inputs: if you use voice logging, you may submit audio to be processed into a meal entry.

We do not store voice recordings on our servers.

Important: Even though we do not store photos or voice recordings, the third-party services that process them (described below) may temporarily process and retain data according to their own policies and settings.

C. Information collected automatically

Device and usage data: app interactions, approximate location derived from IP address, device type, operating system version, app version, and diagnostic data (such as crash logs).

Identifiers: analytics and device identifiers used for performance, debugging, and analytics.

2) How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide the Service (create meal logs, show dashboards, generate nutrition estimates, and display insights).
  • Personalize the Service (goal-based insights, nutrient focus, trends).
  • Improve reliability and performance (analytics, debugging, crash reporting, feature improvement).
  • Provide support and communicate with you (service messages and responses to inquiries).
  • Protect the Service (security, fraud/abuse prevention, enforcing our Terms).

3) AI Processing and Nutrition Estimates

Nouriva uses third-party AI services to analyze information you submit (such as meal text, meal photos, and voice input) to generate structured meal logs and nutrition-related estimates.

What we send for processing: text you enter for meals; meal photos if you choose photo logging; and audio if you choose voice logging.

What we store: we store the results of the processing (for example, detected foods, estimated nutrients, and classifications) so they can appear in your history, dashboard, and insights.

What we do not store: we do not store meal photos or voice recordings on our servers.

Nutrition estimates and insights are informational and may be inaccurate. They are not medical advice.

We share information only as needed to operate the Service:

Service providers (processors): We use third-party providers to host and operate core app infrastructure, analytics, and AI processing. These providers process information on our behalf.

Legal and safety reasons: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, and the Service (e.g., fraud prevention, security incidents).

We do not sell your personal information.

5) Third-Party Providers
